Bridging Space Technology and Egyptian Agriculture

Nitaq ("orbit" in Arabic) represents our mission to bring space-age technology down to Earth. Founded in Egypt, we combine our rich heritage with modern innovation to solve pressing agricultural challenges.

Our system integrates satellite imagery with IoT sensors to monitor vast agricultural areas across the Nile Delta, Western Desert oases, and Sinai Peninsula. This dual approach provides both macro and micro perspectives on crop health and environmental conditions.

With Nitaq, Egyptian farmers gain access to technology that was once available only to large agricultural corporations, leveling the playing field and boosting productivity across the nation.
